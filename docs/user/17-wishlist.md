# Wishlist

Keep a priority list of books you want to add to your library next — separate
from the **Want to Read** reading status, which only applies to books you
already own.

---

## Wishlist vs. "Want to Read"

These are two different things, easy to confuse at first:

| | Wishlist | Want to Read |
|---|---------|--------------|
| Applies to | Books **not yet** in your library | Books **already** catalogued as owned copies |
| Purpose | "I want to buy/borrow this" | "I own it, haven't started it yet" |
| Has a location (room/shelf)? | No | Yes |

Use the Wishlist for books you're planning to get; once you actually own a
copy, add it to your library (see below) — at that point it behaves like any
other book, including reading status.

---

## Adding a Book to Your Wishlist

1. Open **Wishlist** (sidebar)
2. Click **Add to Wishlist**
3. Enter the title, author, and (optionally) an ISBN if you already know it
4. Click **Save**

Wishlist entries are lightweight — they don't need a location, publisher, or
any of the other bibliographic details a catalogued book has.

---

## Prioritizing Your Wishlist

Wishlist items are ranked so you can tell your library what to look for next:

1. Open **Wishlist**
2. Reorder items (drag, or use the priority controls) to reflect what you want most

---

## Turning a Wishlist Item Into a Book

Once you've actually acquired the book:

1. Open **Wishlist**
2. Find the item and click **Add to Library**
3. The **Add Book** form opens, pre-filled with the wishlist entry's title/author
4. Look it up by ISBN if you have one now, or keep the details as entered
5. Assign a location (room → bookcase → shelf) and click **Save**

The item is automatically removed from your wishlist once the book is
successfully added — you don't need to delete it separately.

See **[Managing Your Library → Add From Your Wishlist](03-managing-library.md#method-4-add-from-your-wishlist)**
for the same flow from the library side.

---

## Removing a Wishlist Item

If you've changed your mind about a book:

1. Open **Wishlist**
2. Find the item
3. Click **Remove** (trash icon)

This only removes it from the wishlist — it has no effect on your library
(there's nothing to remove from the catalog, since the item was never
catalogued).

---

## Who Can Manage the Wishlist

Adding, reordering, and removing wishlist items follows the same permissions
as adding books: **Admin** and **Editor** roles can manage it. **Viewers** can
browse the wishlist but can't change it.
