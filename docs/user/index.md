## What is Jinbocho?

Jinbocho is a **home library management system** that helps you and the people you share
a home (or a collection) with keep track of physical books. Named after the famous book
district in Tokyo, it lets you:

- Catalogue every book with title, author, ISBN, and cover
- Know exactly which room and shelf each book is on
- Track reading progress, ratings, and personalised AI recommendations
- Scan a single ISBN, or photograph a whole shelf and let AI read every spine at once
- Search your entire collection instantly
- Keep a wishlist of books you want to add next
- Export your library as CSV or JSON, or import an existing Goodreads library
- Lend books and keep track of who has them

Jinbocho runs in the browser — no installation needed. It works on phones, tablets,
and computers. Every library can have multiple members with different permission
levels, and a single person can belong to more than one library (e.g. their own and
a partner's or a friend's) and switch between them.
